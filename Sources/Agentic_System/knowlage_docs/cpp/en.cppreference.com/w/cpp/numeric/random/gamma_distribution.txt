in header <random> template < class RealType = double > class gamma_distribution ; (since C++11) Produces random positive floating-point values x , distributed according to probability density function: \(\mathsf{p}(x\mid\alpha,\beta) = \frac{e^{-x/\beta} }{\beta^\alpha\cdot\Gamma(\alpha)}\cdot x^{\alpha-1} \) P(x|α,β) = e -x/β β α · Γ(α) · x α-1 where α is known as the shape parameter and β is known as the scale parameter. The shape parameter is sometimes denoted by the letter k and the scale parameter is sometimes denoted by the letter θ . For floating-point α , the value obtained is the sum of α independent exponentially distributed random variables, each of which has a mean of β . std::gamma_distribution satisfies RandomNumberDistribution .
